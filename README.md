## Purpose
These are N8N automations brought to you by Sailing Byte (https://sailingbyte.com). They are meant to make your life easier - for DevOps, Manager or Scrum Master.

Followign automations are included in this repo:
Parse DMARC reports
Noco Kanban Board with AI Prioritization
Daylight Saving Time Notification
Email mailbox as Todoist tasks

You can find all the information about workflows on N8N creator page: https://n8n.io/creators/lukaszpp/

You will find also additional information on Sailing Byte page.
